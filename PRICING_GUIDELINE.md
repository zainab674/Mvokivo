# Enterprise Pricing Guideline

## Overview
This document outlines the pricing structure for the MananUltratalk implementation, including subscription tiers and usage-based costs.

## Subscription Plans

### Starter Plan
*   **Price**: $19/month
*   **Target**: Small businesses getting started with AI voice agents.
*   **Features**:
    *   Basic analytics
    *   Email support
    *   2 team members
    *   Standard integrations
    *   Minutes purchased separately

### Professional Plan (Most Popular)
*   **Price**: $49/month
*   **Target**: Growing teams needing more power.
*   **Features**:
    *   Advanced analytics & reporting
    *   Priority support
    *   10 team members
    *   All integrations
    *   Custom branding
    *   Minutes purchased separately

### Enterprise Plan
*   **Price**: Custom (Contact Sales)
*   **Target**: Large organizations with custom needs.
*   **Features**:
    *   Real-time analytics
    *   24/7 phone support
    *   Unlimited team members
    *   Enterprise integrations
    *   Advanced security
    *   Dedicated account manager
    *   Minutes purchased separately

## Usage Pricing (Minutes)

Minutes for voice agents are purchased separately from the subscription.

*   **Standard Rate**: $0.01 per minute (USD)
*   **Minimum Purchase**: 0 minutes (Pay as you go available)
*   **Bulk Discounts**: Available for Enterprise agreements.

## ROI Estimates
*   **Starter**: Estimated $2,500 monthly ROI
*   **Professional**: Estimated $8,500 monthly ROI
*   **Enterprise**: Estimated $50,000+ monthly ROI

## Notes
*   All plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee.
*   Cancel anytime; no long-term contracts required for Starter and Professional plans.
*   Payments are processed securely via Stripe/Paddle.
